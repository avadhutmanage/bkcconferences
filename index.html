About the College
Established 1975 · Belgaum, Karnataka

D.M.S. Mandal's Bhaurao Kakatkar College has been imparting quality education to students of the border district of Belgaum since 1975. Named after philanthropist Late Shri. Bhaurao Dhanajirao Kakatkar, the college promotes scientific temper, self-reliance, and self-esteem among young people.

The college offers aided UG Courses in Commerce, Arts, Science and self-finance courses including M.Com and M.Sc. Chemistry. Re-accredited at 'A' Grade by NAAC with CGPA 3.11.

About the Seminar

This seminar addresses the profound, multi-dimensional impact of Artificial Intelligence on human structures, research methodologies, and governance. It explores how AI is reshaping social science disciplines such as history, sociology, economics, and political science — moving beyond tech-hype to understand the ethical, legal, and sociological realities of the 21st century.

About the Seminar

The Indian Knowledge System (IKS) offers profound, holistic insights for a world grappling with complex challenges. This seminar, jointly organized by the Departments of Marathi, English, Kannada, and Hindi, serves as a vital interdisciplinary platform to explore how ancient Indian wisdom can be integrated with modern advancements.

About the Conference

ICAPCMSSD 2026 is jointly organized by the Departments of Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics and IQAC. It brings together researchers, academicians, industry experts, and students from across the world to share recent advances in physics, chemistry, and mathematics — focused on sustainable development and global challenges such as energy sustainability, environmental protection, and technological innovation.

Objectives

  • Promote interdisciplinary research in physical, chemical, and mathematical sciences
  • Discuss recent developments contributing to sustainable development
  • Provide a platform for researchers, academicians, and students
  • Encourage collaboration between academia, research institutions, and industry
  • Explore scientific solutions for global environmental and technological challenges
